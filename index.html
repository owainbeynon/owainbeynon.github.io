Research

My research at UCL focusses on developing novel approaches for data driven materials modelling, such as using ontologies and semantic data for AI methods in material science.

Of particular interest is solid-state systems, for example, zeolite catalysts and their properties such as lattice dynamics.

In doing so, I employ a range of modelling techniques such as ab initio methods (e.g. density functional theory, perutbation theory), molecular dynamics simulation, force fields, and hybrid quantum mechanics/ molecular mechanics methods to study the properties of materials.
